# Business Proposal: Cedar Elevators AI & Digital Enhancement Roadmap

**Date:** January 15, 2026  
**Document Type:** Strategic Implementation Plan (Revised)  
**Status:** Proposal v2  

---

## 1. Executive Summary

Following the successful deployment of the **Cedar Storefront (Phase 1)** for a highly efficient cost, this proposal outlines the roadmap for the next stages of growth. 

**Strategic Shift:** Instead of heavy industrial software, we will focus on **smart website enhancements** and **AI-driven automation** that provide immediate value to the sales and support process.

**Budget Goal:** High-impact features with a streamlined budget, keeping total future investment **under ₹10 Lakhs**.

---

## 2. Phase 1: The Foundation (Current Status)

**Objective:** B2B/B2C Digital Sales Channel.  
**Cost:** ~₹35,000 (Completed)  

### Deliverables Live
*   Next.js 16 E-commerce Storefront.
*   Supabase Backend & Authentication.
*   Razorpay Payments & Basic Quote System.
*   Admin Dashboard for Products/Orders.

---

## 3. Phase 2: User Experience & Smart Automation

**Objective:** Enhance the current website to close meaningful gaps and automate manual tasks using cost-effective tools.

### Implementation Scope

#### 2A. Enhanced Customer Experience
*   **Progressive Web App (PWA)**: Turn the website into an installable mobile app for customers (saves cost of building native apps).
*   **Advanced Product Search**: Implement fuzzy search and "Similar Products" recommendations to boost cross-selling.
*   **Abandoned Cart Recovery**: Automated email sequences to recover lost sales.

#### 2B. Process Automation
*   **Automated Invoice Generation**: Auto-generate PDF invoices and email them upon payment success (reducing manual admin work).
*   **WhatsApp Notification Integration**: Send order updates and quote approvals directly to customer WhatsApp (using low-cost APIs).

### Budget & Timeline
*   **Estimated Duration:** 1.5 - 2 Months
*   **Estimated Budget:** **₹ 1,50,000 - ₹ 2,00,000 INR**

---

## 4. Phase 3: AI-Driven Sales & Support

**Objective:** Integrate AI to reduce support workload and assist customers 24/7.

### Implementation Scope

#### 3A. AI Sales Assistant (Chatbot)
*   **Context-Aware Bot**: Integrate an AI assistant (OpenAI/Claude API) trained on Cedar's product data.
    *   *Capabilities:* Answer technical questions ("What is the capacity of the Monarch controller?"), guide users to products, and troubleshoot basic errors.
    *   *Human Handoff:* Seamlessly escalate to a human admin if the bot cannot answer.

#### 3B. Smart Quote Analysis
*   **AI Price Estimator**: Use historical data to suggest optimal pricing for custom quotes to the Admin.
*   **Lead Scoring**: AI analyzes incoming quotes and flags High-Priority B2B clients based on company data and order value.

### Budget & Timeline
*   **Estimated Duration:** 2 - 3 Months
*   **Estimated Budget:** **₹ 2,50,000 - ₹ 3,50,000 INR**

---

## 5. Phase 4: Data Intelligence & Expansion

**Objective:** detailed analytics and expansion of the platform's capabilities.

### Implementation Scope

#### 4A. Advanced Analytics Dashboard
*   **Business Intelligence**: Visual charts for "Most Sold Products," "Customer Retention Rate," and "Revenue by Region."
*   **Inventory Forecasting**: Simple algorithms to alert when stock is low based on sales velocity.

#### 4B. Multi-Region Capability
*   **Smart Localization**: Prepare the platform for expansion to other states/countries (currency toggle, basic language support).
*   **Vendor Portal Lite**: A dedicated section for third-party vendors to view their assigned orders (if applicable).

### Budget & Timeline
*   **Estimated Duration:** 2 - 3 Months
*   **Estimated Budget:** **₹ 3,00,000 - ₹ 4,00,000 INR**
