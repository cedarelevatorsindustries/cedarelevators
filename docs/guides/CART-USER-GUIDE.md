## üìç Cart Features Explained

### Profile Switching

If you have a business account, you can maintain **two separate carts**:

1. **Individual Cart** - Personal purchases
2. **Business Cart** - Company purchases

**How to switch:**
1. Click profile badge (top right)
2. Select "Shopping as: Individual" or "Shopping as: Business"
3. Cart automatically switches
4. Both carts are saved separately

**Why separate carts?**
- Different pricing (individual vs business rates)
- Different billing addresses
- Different invoicing requirements
- Tax purposes

---

### Cart Locking During Checkout

When you proceed to checkout, your cart is **soft-locked** for 5 minutes.

**What this means:**
- ‚ö†Ô∏è You'll see a warning: "Cart is in checkout mode"
- You can still modify items (but not recommended)
- Changes during checkout may affect your order
- Lock automatically expires after 5 minutes

**Why we do this:**
- Prevents accidental modifications during payment
- Ensures order matches what you're paying for
- Reduces errors and refunds

**How to unlock manually:**
- Go back to cart page
- Lock expires automatically
- Or click "Unlock Cart" if available

---

### Guest Cart Merge

**Scenario:** You added items as a guest, then signed in.

**What happens automatically:**
1. Your guest cart items are detected
2. Items are added to your user cart
3. If you already have the same product:
   - Quantities are **added together**
   - Example: Guest cart (2x Widget) + User cart (3x Widget) = 5x Widget
4. Guest cart is cleared from browser
5. You see notification: "X items added to your cart"

**Zero data loss:**
- All your guest items are preserved
- Stock availability is checked
- Unavailable items are reported

---

### Dynamic Pricing

**Important:** Prices are **always current**!

**How it works:**
- Prices are NOT saved in your cart
- When you view cart, we fetch latest prices
- If admin changes price, you see new price
- You always pay current market rate

**Why this matters:**
- No "old price" vs "new price" confusion
- Fair pricing for everyone
- No expired cart pricing issues

**Price updates:**
- Automatic on every cart view
- Reflected immediately
- You'll see notification if major change

---

### Stock Availability

Each cart item shows real-time stock status:

- ‚úÖ **In Stock** - Green checkmark
- ‚ö†Ô∏è **Low Stock** - Yellow warning
- ‚ùå **Out of Stock** - Red badge

**If item goes out of stock:**
1. You'll see "Out of Stock" badge
2. Checkout button is disabled
3. Options:
   - Remove item from cart
   - Request quote (we'll arrange stock)
   - Wait for restock (add to wishlist)

---

### Cart Abandonment

If you don't use your cart for 30 days:

- Cart is marked as "abandoned"
- Items are NOT deleted
- You can restore it anytime
- Just sign in and your cart will be back

## ‚ùì Common Questions

### Q: Why can't I see prices?

**A:** You need to be:
- Signed in as Individual user, OR
- Signed in as Verified Business user

Guest users and unverified business users cannot see prices.

---

### Q: Why is checkout disabled for me?

**A:** Only **Verified Business** users can checkout directly.

Individual users and unverified business users must:
- Request a quote instead
- Our team will send formal quotation
- You can approve and proceed via email/phone

---

### Q: What happens to my cart if I sign out?

**A:** Your cart is **permanently saved** to your account.
- Sign back in anytime
- Cart will be exactly as you left it
- Items, quantities all preserved

---

### Q: Can I have multiple carts?

**A:** Yes, if you have a business account:
- Individual Cart (personal shopping)
- Business Cart (company shopping)

Switch between them using profile selector.

---

### Q: My cart shows "Cart is in checkout mode" - what do I do?

**A:** This means:
- You (or another session) started checkout
- Cart is soft-locked for 5 minutes
- You can still modify items (not recommended)
- Wait for lock to expire, or complete checkout

**To unlock:**
- Complete the checkout
- Wait 5 minutes
- Or contact support if stuck

---

### Q: Item in my cart is now "Out of Stock" - what can I do?

**Options:**

1. **Request Quote**
   - We can arrange stock for you
   - Usually 7-14 days lead time

2. **Remove Item**
   - Continue with available items

3. **Add to Wishlist**
   - Get notified when back in stock

---

### Q: Price changed since I added item - why?

**A:** Prices are **always current**.
- We don't lock prices in cart
- You see live pricing
- Ensures fairness and transparency

If price increased significantly, you'll see a notification.

---

### Q: How do I clear my entire cart?

1. Go to Cart page
2. Scroll to bottom
3. Click "Clear Cart" button
4. Confirm action

All items will be removed immediately.

---

### Q: Can I save my cart for later?

**A:** Your cart is **automatically saved**!
- For signed-in users: Permanently saved
- For guests: Saved in browser (until you clear cookies)

**To save permanently:**
- Sign in or create account
- Cart is automatically saved to your profile

---

### Q: I added items as guest, then signed in - where did they go?

**A:** They were **automatically merged**!
- Check your cart badge (top right)
- You should see increased count
- All guest items are now in your user cart
- You received notification: "X items added to cart"
